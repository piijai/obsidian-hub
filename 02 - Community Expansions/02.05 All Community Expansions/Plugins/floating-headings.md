# Floating Headings

Plugin ID: `floating-headings`
Links: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/k0src/Floating-Headings-Obsidian-Plugin) or [<button id=HH>Open in Obsidian</button>](obsidian://show-plugin?id=floating-headings)
Developed by: [[k0src]]
Mobile compatible: [[Mobile-compatible plugins|Yes]]

Displays a floating, collapsible outline of a note's headings on the right side of the editor. Expands on hover, click to navigate.
